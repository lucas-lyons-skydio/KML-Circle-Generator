📍 KML Circle Generator

How to use:
  1. Enter your locations (name + full address)
  2. Set the circle radius in miles
  3. Click "Geocode Addresses" to convert addresses to coordinates
  4. Click "Generate KML" to download your file
  5. Open the KML in Google Earth or import into Google My Maps
